Prototype Industries Inc.

User Experience Intern

Overview

When I was brought on as an intern in June of 2019, my task was to identify usability issues and propose solutions to make their patented software, Skywriter, more user friendly, for both internal and external users.

My Role

Research, UX & UI Design, Prototyping

Methods - Research, Interviews, Usability Testing, User Flows, Wireframes, Higher-fidelity Mockups

Client

Prototype Industries inc. is a technical publishing company, creating aircraft manuals for leading manufacturers such as panasonic, thales, boeing etc.. The close knit group of engineers, project managers, and illustrators are dedicated to keeping people flying safely by meticulously and accurately documenting safety procedures.
